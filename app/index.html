Face Mouse


Shortcuts

Ctrl/cmd + spacebar moves cursor to centre of screen
Ctrl/cmd + (1-4) moves cursor to centre of one of the four quadrants of your screen
Shift+esc pauses/starts video and tracking

Settings

Sensitivity: (Range: 1 - 50, default: 6)
 How much the mouse moves in relation to the tracked points.

 Delay: (Range: 1 - 100, default: 0)
 Cursor delay in milliseconds.

 Jitter reduction threshold: (Range: 0 - 0.003, default: 0.0012)
Ignores movement under a certain threshold to reduce jitter.
